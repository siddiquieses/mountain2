Types/styles



Types of Mountaineering

Mountaineering is divided into different styles based on terrain, techniques, and equipment used. Below are the main types:


Alpine Mountaineering

Fast-paced climbing with minimal equipment. Popular in the European Alps.

Expedition Mountaineering

Involves large groups, multiple camps, and heavy loads for high-altitude climbs.

Ice Climbing

Climbing frozen waterfalls and glaciers using ice axes and crampons.

Rock Climbing

Climbing natural rock formations or artificial climbing walls.

Ski Mountaineering

Combining skiing and climbing to ascend and descend mountains.
