Mountaineering Sheltering

Survive in extreme conditions with the right sheltering techniques

Types of Shelters

Tent Shelters

Portable and easy to set up. Ideal for all-weather camping.

Cave Shelters

Natural rock shelters providing protection against harsh weather.

Snow Shelters

Insulated igloos and snow caves that retain body heat.

Lean-to Shelter

Simple structure made with logs and leaves for quick protection.

Hammock Shelter

Suspended above ground, ideal for tropical and wet environments.

Tarp Shelter

Lightweight, flexible, and easy to set up for temporary coverage.

Setting Up a Shelter in Extreme Conditions

Choosing the Right Location

Look for level ground, away from avalanche-prone areas.

Protection from Wind

Use rocks or snow walls to block strong winds.

Insulation & Warmth

Use ground insulation and layer your shelter to retain heat.

Emergency Precautions

Always keep an emergency exit and signal devices handy.
