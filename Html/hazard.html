Mountaineering Hazards

Stay safe in extreme conditions by understanding risks and safety measures

Common Hazards in Mountaineering

Avalanches

Sudden snow slides can be deadly. Stay alert and use avalanche safety gear.

Altitude Sickness

Low oxygen levels cause nausea, headache, and dizziness. Acclimatize properly.

Falls & Slips

Loose rocks and ice increase fall risk. Always secure yourself with ropes.

Safety Measures & First Aid

Avalanche Safety

Check avalanche forecasts, wear beacons, and carry probes & shovels.

Altitude Sickness Prevention

Acclimatize slowly, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol & smoking at high altitudes.

First Aid for Falls

Use splints for fractures, stop bleeding with bandages, and seek medical help immediately.
